# About Monzo Bank

Monzo is a UK-based digital bank that aims to make money work for everyone by offering intuitive, transparent, and customer-centric banking experiences. 

Known for its mobile-first approach, Monzo empowers users with real-time financial insights, budgeting tools, and accessible support, all delivered through a friendly, human-centered digital interface.

Monzo was founded with the mission to transform traditional banking into a more open, fair, and usable system. 

By leveraging technology, Monzo reduces complexity, increases transparency, and builds trust with its community. The brand's tone, design, and service model reflect its commitment to financial well-being and inclusivity.
